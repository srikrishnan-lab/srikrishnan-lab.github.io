Join Us

We are an inclusive, interdisciplinary group working at the interface of engineering, earth science, data science, economics, and decision science. Join us to work on managing climate risk across a variety of sectors and scales. Students from underrepresented groups and first-generation students are particularly encouraged to apply.

Before applying, please visit the lab manual to make sure that you're comfortable with our mission, values, expectations, and policies.
