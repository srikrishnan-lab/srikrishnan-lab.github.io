About

Vivek Srikrishnan is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Biological & Environmental Engineering at Cornell. He works on climate risk management, and is particularly interested in how uncertainties and multi-sector feedbacks can influence the efficacy of risk management interventions. He also lost on Jeopardy!.
